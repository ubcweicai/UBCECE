Wei Cai    Ph.D.   蔡玮

Former Ph.D. Student and Postdoc at UBC

Thank you for visiting my research page in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of British Columbia, Vancouver. From September 2011 to April 2016, I was pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Wireless Network and Mobile System Laboratory (WiNMoS) under the supervision of Prof. Victor C.M. Leung. I also completed two year postdoctoral research in WiNMoS in 2018.

I am currently an assistant professor in School of Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen).

To know more about my recent updates, please visit my new home page: Home@CUHK-Shenzhen.
